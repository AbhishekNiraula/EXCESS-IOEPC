X-HACK 2.0

Tech enthusiasts, get ready to unleash your creativity! EXCESS proudly presents the pinnacle event of our annual technical festival: the X-Tech 4.0 Green Technology Hardware Hackathon! In this thrilling 28-hour challenge, immerse yourself in crafting hardware prototypes that address real-world problems, all within the realm of sustainable and eco-friendly technology.

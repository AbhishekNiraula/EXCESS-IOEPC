About The Event

Xtech Studio, is more than an event, rather a technical festival which encompasses of all the technical events that are inevitable to be known as an Engineering student. Going into the tenth year of establishment of EXCESS Society, here we come with the third installment of Xtech title "Xtech Studio 3.0". Xtech is filled with lots of technical events such as Natural Language Processing Workshop, Satellite and Communication Workshop, Cyber security and Blockchain Webinar, IOT Training, MATLAB Simulation Training and Competition, PCB Designing Training and Competition, Robotic Event, Hackathon, Project Demonstration complemented by Quizzes, Seminars and Webinars. Pre Events have already begun and we're heading to out Main Event to be conducted at Poush 20,21,22 2079. EXCESS Has always been following the ideology of learning and teaching with utmost generosity and sense of brotherhood. The Even Xtech Studio 3.0 is expected to bring a cordial involvement of above 10000 students altogether to cherish our motive and make it a grand success.

Event Venue

Event venue location info and gallery

Institute Of Engineering Purwanchal Campus, Dharan

Purwanchal Campus, formerly known as Eastern Region (ERC) Campus is one of constituent campuses of Tribhuvan University (TU) and one of the associate engineering campuses of Institute of Engineering (IOE) which is a comprehensive, non-profit making institution and pioneering institution of higher education level in Nepal funded by Government of Nepal.

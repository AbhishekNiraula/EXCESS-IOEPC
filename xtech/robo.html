Robotics Competition

Get ready for the ultimate competition! Gear up with your racing bot because EXCESS is proud to announce "Robo-Race" as one of the main events at the upcoming X-Tech Studio 3.0 in Association with Robotics Club IOEPC. With our increasing reliance on technology, it's more important than ever to understand how robots work. That's why we're giving students the chance to learn from each other and compete in a fun and educational way. So, are you ready to put your engineering skills to the test? Assemble your team (minimum of three, maximum of four people) and get ready to build a robot that's both fast and controlled. You never know what challenges you might face, so it's important to be prepared. Join us for Robo-Race and see what you're capable of! Entry Fee: Rs. 1000 per team. Prize Pool: Rs. 45000 Venue: IOE - Purwanchal Campus Dharan Event: 28th and 29th Poush
